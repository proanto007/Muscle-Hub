Abdominals

Crunches

Difficulty: Beginner

  1. Lie flat on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground, about a foot from your lower back.
  2. Place your fingertips on your temples with your palms facing out.
  3. Engage your core, raise your head and shoulders off the floor, then return to the starting position. Repeat.

Leg Raises

Difficulty: Beginner

  1. Lie on your back with your arms at your sides, palms down.
  2. Keep your legs straight and together.
  3. Raise your legs to a 90° angle, pause, then slowly lower them back down.
  4. Move slowly to avoid using momentum, ensuring better muscle engagement.

Plank

Difficulty: Beginner

  1. Place your forearms on the ground, elbows bent at 90°, aligned beneath your shoulders.
  2. Keep your feet together, balancing on your toes.
  3. Engage your core, forming a straight line from heels to head. Hold the position.

Russian Twist

Difficulty: Intermediate

  1. Sit on the floor with knees bent at a 90° angle.
  2. Keep your feet hovering above the ground (or rest heels if needed).
  3. Rotate your upper body from side to side, engaging your obliques.
